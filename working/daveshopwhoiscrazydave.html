About Crazy Dave

Have you ever seen a guy riding busily around Kennesaw on a unicycle? You just met Crazy Dave, aka Dave Dean. In addition to running his hair studio, Dave is an avid unicycler, believes in fitness and the environment, has been featured in several articles and was even a contestant on Animal Planet's King Of The Jungle reality show!

Fitness

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Dave used to be a personal trainer, so he is a big believer in personal fitness. Although he no longer works as a trainer, he tries to ride his unicycle in an effort to inspire other people to get out there a bit more.

Environment

Cycling, whether on one wheel or two, is a great way to get around town. Even if you live too far away to go to and from home on a cycle, you can do your part by simply walking or biking instead of driving the next time you need to run an errand. You'll be glad you did.
