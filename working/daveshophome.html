Welcome to Crazy Dave's Hair Studio!

At Crazy Dave's Hair Studio, we have a great passion for hair. We love to bring out the natural beauty of your hair with custom cuts & colors. We are a family so when you come in you'll see the same stylist every time. We welcome you to call or stop by for an appointment today!

Our friendly staff is always ready to serve our customers! Come on in and check us out!

And remember the Crazy Dave guarantee: At Crazy Dave's if you don't love the cut, we will shave your head for free!
