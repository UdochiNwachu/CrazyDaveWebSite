Dave in the News!

Crazy Kennesaw State University Interview

This month the editors at Kennesaw State university thought it was time to catch up with ol' Dave and see what the fuss was all about. The interview took place around the time Crazy Dave was volunteering with students in the KSU computer science department for a web development project. Not only were the editors impressed with Dave's part in working with students design a website for Crazy Dave's Hair Studio at KSU, they were equally impressed with his community activism and his unique talent on the unicycle. Dave is very proud to contribute to student's success at KSU and encourages them take part in community activism to promote the well being of their surrounding area. The project Dave is working on with Computer Science students will allow the students real life experience in designing websites for organizations.

MDJ interviews Dave

This week the Marietta Daily Journal conducted an interview with Dave. His notoriety on the cycle and off the cycle earned him a spot in the MDJ. The article focused on Dave's unique flair to cycling and his community outreach. The interview revealed the story of Dave's encounter with Animal Planet as well as how his hobby has taken a life of it's own.
